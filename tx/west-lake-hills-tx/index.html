West Lake Hills, TX Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

West Lake Hills (locally referred to as 'Westlake') is a city in Travis County, Texas, United States. The population was 3,444 at the 2020 census. The city developed rapidly in the 1970s and 1980s on the south side of the Colorado River. According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 3.7 square miles (9.6 km2).
